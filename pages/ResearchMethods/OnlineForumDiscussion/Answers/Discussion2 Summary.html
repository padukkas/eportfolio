Discussion 2: Discussion 2: Accuracy of information - Summary

Summary Post

With a careful analysis of the given scenario along with the different viewpoints and responses of my fellow colleagues, it can be stated that both accuracy and ethics play a significant role when it comes to the overall standard and outcome of a research.

As a result of Ethical norms being assisted in establishing the aims of a research, a researcher should adhere to the ethical norms in order to establish the truth by avoiding errors which can occur due to misrepresenting research data based on ethical conditions.

Therefore, a researcher should always adopt ethical norms while avoiding those ethical concerns affecting the scientific truth. This will indeed ensure the accountability of researchers and their work to the public at large.

In a legal format, abandoning ethical concerns falls under the category of “research misconduct;” this includes Fabrication, Falsification and Plagiarism. In order to avoid these misconducts researchers can always seek aid from both jurisdictional and international research review bords, available codes and legislations like (Resnik, 2020),

  • Research Ethics Board (REB)
  • Singapore Statement on Research Integrity
  • American Chemical Society, The Chemist Professional’s Code of Conduct
  • Code of Ethics (American Society for Clinical Laboratory Science)
  • Statement on Professional Ethics (American Association of University Professors)
  • Nuremberg Code

Finally, the given scenario can be summarized as,

The reviewer should predict the types of bias that can impact the outcome of his report and should draw conclusions without ignoring the scientific truth. And to derive the most accurate and logical result, it is crucial to investigate similar studies along with adopted methodologies. Further, it can be stated that Abi is entitled to conduct the review in an ethical manner by forwarding genuine results even if the results indicate negative impact on the manufacturer.
