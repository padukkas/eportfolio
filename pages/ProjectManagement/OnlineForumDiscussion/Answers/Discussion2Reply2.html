Factors Affecting User Experience

Your post was an interesting read. I do agree with your explanation on how human emotional responses towards a specific product can be influenced by factors like educational background, financial affordability, and peer reviews etc. Your choice of example and the explanation referring to the CUE Model guides the reader in a clear direction to understand the discussed content.

However, when addressing product-related emotions, as we know from the visual aspect to the use and function, users experience numerous emotions and I do think addressing this issue while referring to the aspects of “initial stage” and “later stage” highlighting “Hedonic halo effect (attractive is usable)” and “Pragmatic halo effect (usable gets attractive)” (Minge and Thüring, 2018) can further strengthen the flow of your explanation.

References:

Dawson, W. (2019). Reasons Why Emotions Play An Important Role in UX Design. [online] CODERSERA. Available at: https://codersera.com/blog/role-of-emotions-in-ux-design/#:~:text=User%20experience%20(UX)%20is%20a [Accessed 12 Aug. 2022].

Minge, M. and Thüring, M. (2018). Hedonic and pragmatic halo effects at early stages of User Experience. International Journal of Human-Computer Studies, 109, pp.13–25. doi:10.1016/j.ijhcs.2017.07.007.
