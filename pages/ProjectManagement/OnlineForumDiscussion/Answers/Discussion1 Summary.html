Introduction:

Project management is the process of leading the work of a team to achieve project goals with the given tools, budgets and team. The key points to have a successful project is to, (University of Minnesota, 2015)

  • Good planning
  • Organizing
  • Leading
  • Controlling

After going over all the posts of my peers and their analysis of failed projects, it is far more evident why being successful at project management is very important if you want to have a successful project lifecycle.

References:
