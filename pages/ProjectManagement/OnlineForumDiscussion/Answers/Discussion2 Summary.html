Discussion 2: Factors Affecting User Experience - Summary

Summary Post

When analyzing articles of my colleges and replies that I got for my Initial post, it can be stated that, when we consider human emotional responses towards a specific product apart from visual aspect and experience the human emotional responses towards a specific product can be influenced by factors like users’ educational background, financial affordability, and peer reviews etc. Therefore, it may vary from person to person. This can be highlighted through the following statement.

“One cannot design a user experience, only design for a user experience. In particular, one cannot design a sensual experience, but only create the design features that can evoke it.” -Professor Jeff Johnson, -

Further, it is evident that the provided CUE model is silent when addressing the user experience covering all users. As for an example, user experience can also be affected by factors relating to prior experiences with products from the same family or the brand.

Therefore, in conclusion it can be stated that similar to emotions, users' interest also can vary from one user to another. Therefore, at the end of the day user experience designers cannot control how the product is being used by an individual, how that person moves or commands while using the product, the designers only can control the visual effects and the overall system.
