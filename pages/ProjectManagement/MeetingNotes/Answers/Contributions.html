Individual contributions to the projects carried out during the module

Question:

Your individual contributions to the projects carried out during the module, including those submitted in Units 6 and 11

Answer:

For unit 6 submission design document I was able to contribute ideas on how we can implement some of the requirements that was given to use by Group 2. Also helped in the development of the Project Report to document the approach of our application development.

For unit 11 submission I worked as a developer of the code. I worked on developing the authentication and authorization using the Firebase framework, which required me to connect the firebase APIs to our application and to wire the front end of the application to pass the user login/register information back to firebase to store, authenticate and authorize.

For unit 11 submission I also worked on the presentation deck regarding firebase and the methodology we used which was Waterfall, I added some visuals to make it easier for the reader to understand the concepts. Also helped Ian to present during the presentation with the Professor.
