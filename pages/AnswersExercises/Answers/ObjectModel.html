Object Model Diagram Output

This diagram represents a object model for an Online Super Market. An assumption I made was that this super market is fully online.

To begin this process, the customer shops for products and add said items to their shopping carts. The admin user can reset password and unlock locked customer accounts which is why there is a override between the customer and the admin user. When the customer add a product to their shopping cart, the system checks if their is enough items in stock for other customers, if not the floor staff will restock said shelf.

The customer can have a loyalty card, if they do infact have the loyalty points card the customer can use that as a method of payment.

Once the customer is ready to checkout, this will trigger the payment method object, asking the customer to chose their method of payment. After the customer completes the payment, they are given a tracking number for the items. Now the delivery staff collects the items which will update the order status object and this will relay back to the customer showing the most updated information.
