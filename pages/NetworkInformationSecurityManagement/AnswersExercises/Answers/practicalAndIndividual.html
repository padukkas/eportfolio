Introduction:

For this nslookup, WHOIS and traceroute was used to carry out the scanning excerices at allmytype.co.uk website

1) How many hops from your machine to your assigned website?

traceroute was used to count the total hops to reach the websie which was 15 hops.

2) Which step causes the biggest delay in the route? What is the average duration of that delay?

The biggest delay that was noticed was for the network communication between ffm-b5-link.telia.net server. The average duration of that delay was roughly around 350ms.
